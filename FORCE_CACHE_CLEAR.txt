==============================================================================
FORCE CACHE CLEAR - MANUAL STEPS
==============================================================================

Your browser is holding onto old cached data. Follow these exact steps:

METHOD 1: Chrome DevTools (RECOMMENDED - 100% EFFECTIVE)
---------------------------------------------------------
1. Open the PWA: http://localhost:3000/
2. Press F12 (open Developer Tools)
3. Go to "Application" tab (top menu)
4. On left sidebar, click "Service Workers"
5. Check the box "Update on reload"
6. Click "Unregister" next to any service worker
7. On left sidebar, click "Storage"
8. Click "Clear site data" button
9. Close DevTools (F12)
10. Hard reload: Ctrl + Shift + R (or Ctrl + F5)

METHOD 2: Manual Cache Clear
-----------------------------
1. Close ALL browser windows running localhost:3000
2. Press Ctrl + Shift + Delete
3. Select:
   - Time range: "All time"
   - Check: "Cached images and files"
   - Check: "Cookies and other site data"
4. Click "Clear data"
5. Close browser completely
6. Reopen and go to: http://localhost:3000/

METHOD 3: Incognito Window (QUICKEST FOR TESTING)
--------------------------------------------------
1. Press Ctrl + Shift + N
2. Go to: http://localhost:3000/
3. This will show v18 data immediately
